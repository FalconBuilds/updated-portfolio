My Top 3 movies of all-time

Avengers: Endgame

This comes in at number 1 as it is the combination of all the MCU movies so far, bringing a huge, exciting and climatic end to this phase of the MCU.

The Lego Movie

This is just a fun film to watch whenever there is nothing on the TV. It is very nostalgic for any 90's kids who loved lego. Chris Pratt is great.

The Italian Job - 2003

I think its a great reimagining of the original Italian job, Mark Wahlburg does a great job of leading a ruff and ready team into a revenge heist
